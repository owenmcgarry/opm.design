Tattoos: All tattoos are done using traditional hand poking techniques. My work is generally inspired by New England and Atlantic Canadian maritime folk art. While, historically, tattoos were no doubt prevalent, little evidence remains. So instead I look to scrimshaw, engravings, etchings, furniture, needlework, architecture, and paintings.

Hand Poked Tattoos & Flash zine: A selection of pages from a zine I produced to demonstrate my work. It also included some of the photographs above. See the full zine here. 2015, Wellington, NZ.

Flash: A series of drawings made with the intent of being tattooed.

Tattooed orange: Instructions on how to eat an orange, for an unfinished project by my friend Adriana Gallo. Hand poked tattoo on an orange. 2015, Wellington, NZ.
