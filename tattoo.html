Tattoos: All tattoos are done using traditional hand poking techniques. My work is generally inspired by New England and Atlantic Canadian maritime folk art. While, historically, tattoos were no doubt prevalent, little evidence remains. So instead I look to scrimshaw, engravings, etchings, furniture, needlework, architecture, and paintings. Ongoing series.

Flash: A series of drawings made with the intent of being tattooed. Ongoing series.

Hand Poked Tattoos & Flash zines: A selection of pages from a series of zines I produced to demonstrate my work. Zines also included photographs of tattoos. See Volume 1 here. 2015, Wellington, NZ. 2017, Boston, MA.

Tattooed orange: Instructions on how to eat an orange, for Adriana Gallo's project peelorang.es. Hand poked tattoo on an orange. 2015, Wellington, NZ.

Sticked and Stoned: Video from a show I did with Emmaline Payette and others at The Mess in Brooklyn. 2016.
