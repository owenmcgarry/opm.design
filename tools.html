Shipwright Tools: Steel bevel gauge and caulking hook. Made of the same stock with identical dimensions, reducing manufacturing processes and allowing parts to be interchangeable. Both tools can be disassembled simply for maintenance and care. 2013, London, UK.

Hand Tool for Deep Space Missions: A tool handle designed in coöperation with NASA for use in future deep space missions. Designed as a collet system to allow the handle to function with many different tools, reducing weight and volume by having a single handle for multiple tools. Made of titanium, carbon fiber and delryn. 2013, Providence, RI.
