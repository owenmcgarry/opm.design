Someone once accused me of being not a real person; rather, an amalgamation of literary allusions and poetic themes.

I live and work in Somerville Massachusetts, where I am currently employed as a woodworker. I graduated in 2014 with a BFA in industrial design from RISD and studied product and furniture design at Kingston University in London. I've worked as a homing pigeon trainer for artist Duke Riley on his project Fly by Night. I was in charge of the workshop for furniture designer Duncan Sargent in Wellington NZ. I've worked as a shipwright for Gannon & Benjamin on Martha's Vineyard, I've also worked in collaboration with NASA on microgravity workshop environments, and as a monitor in the metal shop at RISD. I have tens of thousands of hours of workshop experience.
